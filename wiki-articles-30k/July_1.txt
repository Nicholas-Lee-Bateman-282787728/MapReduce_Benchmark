       The end of this day marks the halfway point of a leap year. It was the first comet analyzed using polarimetry, by François Arago. 1837 &ndash; A system of civil registration of births, marriages and deaths is established in England and Wales. 1855 &ndash; Signing of the Quinault Treaty: The Quinault and the Quileute cede their land to the United States. 1858 &ndash; Joint reading of Charles Darwin and Alfred Russel Wallace's papers on evolution to the Linnean Society in London. 1862 &ndash; The Russian State Library is founded as the Library of the Moscow Public Museum. 1862   &ndash; Princess Alice of the United Kingdom, second daughter of Queen Victoria, marries Prince Louis of Hesse, the future Louis IV, Grand Duke of Hesse. 1862   &ndash; American Civil War: The Battle of Malvern Hill takes place. Macdonald is sworn in as the first Prime Minister of Canada.

The storm wave sweeps inland, taking with it 20,000 ships and thousands of people. There are 51 deaths in Arkansas alone, 76 deaths and over 400 injuries in all. 1936 &ndash; In Berlin, Germany and Japan sign the Anti-Comintern Pact, agreeing to consult on measures "to safeguard their common interests" in the case of an unprovoked attack by the Soviet Union against either nation.

   Ocean Township is a township located in east central Monmouth County, New Jersey, United States. Ocean Township consists of three unincorporated communities including Wanamassa, Oakhurst and Wayside. The township is divided into two zip codes, 07755 and 07712. As of the 2010 United States Census, the township's population was 27,291, reflecting an increase of 332 (+1.2%) from the 26,959 counted in the 2000 Census, which had in turn increased by 1,901 (+7.6%) from the 25,058 counted in the 1990 Census.Table 7. The Township Committee is elected at large by the voters in nonpartisan elections to serve four-year terms of office on a concurrent basis.2012 New Jersey Legislative District Data Book, Rutgers University Edward J. Bloustein School of Planning and Public Policy, March 2013, p. 58. At an annual reorganization meeting, the Township Committee selects one of its members to serve as Mayor. The five-member Council sets policy and adopts local ordinances while the Council-appointed Township Manager oversees the day-to-day administration of the Township.

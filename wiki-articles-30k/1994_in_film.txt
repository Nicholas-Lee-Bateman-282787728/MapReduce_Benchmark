  This is a list of films released in 1994. The top worldwide grosser was Disney's The Lion King, becoming the highest-grossing animated film at the time, although it was slightly overtaken at the North American domestic box office by Forrest Gump, which won the Academy Award for Best Picture and a second consecutive Academy Award for Best Actor for Tom Hanks. Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer celebrated its 70th Anniversary in 1994.

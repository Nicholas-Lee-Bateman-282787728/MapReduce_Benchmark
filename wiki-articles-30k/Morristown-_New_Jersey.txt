According to the United States Census Bureau, Morristown had a total area of 3.026&nbsp;square miles (7.839&nbsp;km2), including 2.929&nbsp;square miles (7.587&nbsp;km2) of land and 0.097&nbsp;square miles (0.252&nbsp;km2) of water (3.22%). The downtown shopping and business district of Morristown is centered around a square park, known as the Morristown Green. It is a former market square from Morristown's colonial days. Morristown has a humid continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dfa/Dfb). The Morristown Town Council consists of seven members: three members elected at-large representing the entire town; and four members representing each of the town's four wards. Members are elected to four-year terms of office on a staggered basis; there is an election every two years, either for the four ward seats or for the at-large and mayoral seats.
